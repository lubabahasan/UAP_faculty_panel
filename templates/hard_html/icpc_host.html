Proud Host of ICPC 2022 World Finals

Welcome to Dhaka, Bangladesh

The 45th International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) World Finals was held in Dhaka from November 6–10, 2022. It was a moment of pride for the entire nation as Bangladesh hosted one of the most prestigious programming competitions in the world.

Meet the Host: University of Asia Pacific (UAP)

Founded in 1996, the University of Asia Pacific (UAP) is a respected private university in Bangladesh, known for academic excellence and innovation in education. UAP has a strong focus on engineering, technology, and computer science — making it an ideal host for the ICPC World Finals.

UAP’s mission is to create future-ready graduates who can compete globally in technology and innovation. Its faculty, students, and administration are all committed to fostering a strong culture of research, ethics, and leadership.

Exceptional Event Management by UAP

Organizing a world-class event like ICPC requires vision, coordination, and dedication — and UAP delivered on every front. From managing international delegations to ensuring high-speed technical support and logistics, the university displayed top-notch organizational capabilities.

  • Seamless coordination with the ICT Division and Bangladesh Computer Council
  • Hospitality and accommodation for participants from over 70 countries
  • Highly efficient volunteer teams made up of UAP students and staff
  • Technical support teams ensured smooth contest operations and fair judging

Dedicated Support from CSE Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at UAP played a vital role behind the scenes. Their tireless dedication, problem-solving expertise, and volunteer spirit contributed significantly to the success of ICPC 2022.

From contest logistics and technical setup to managing data flow and on-the-ground troubleshooting, the CSE faculty and students were everywhere — and they were exceptional.

  • Over 100 student volunteers from CSE worked during the event
  • Provided 24/7 tech support and contestant assistance
  • Managed registration, equipment setup, and coordination
  • Faculty members led the volunteer teams and oversaw technical readiness

This achievement is a proud milestone for the CSE Department of UAP — proving their capability, teamwork, and global-standard professionalism.

Why UAP Deserves Recognition

Hosting ICPC is no small feat — it’s a symbol of excellence in academics, logistics, and global cooperation. UAP rose to the occasion with pride and professionalism, leaving a lasting impression on participants and dignitaries alike.

This event not only showcased UAP's capability but also put Bangladesh on the global map of competitive programming and tech innovation.

Bravo, UAP! You’ve made the nation proud.
